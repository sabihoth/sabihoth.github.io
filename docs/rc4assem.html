Experience / RC4 Assembler

I worked at at RC4 wireless for a little over 3 years, my first year being an assembler. During my time as an assembler I mastered soldering with a soldering iron as well as doing advanced repairs with hot plates and a hot air tool. I did microprocessor swaps and did complex repairs on parts all the way down to 0402 size. I also worked in multiple high stress timelines doing repairs for customers. I stayed at RC4 for 2 additional years as a production manager.


I gained practical experience in the following:

  • Soldering Iron
  • Hot Plate
  • Hot Air tool
  • Advanced repairs including microprocessor swaps
