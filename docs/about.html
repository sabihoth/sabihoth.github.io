About Me

My name is Sebastian Hoth and I am a Senior studying Electrical Engineering with a specialization in ASIC and Embedded systems at NC State. Wile attending Wake Tech I worked for an electronics firm in Raleigh NC called RC4 wireless. I worked there for three years starting as an assembler and working my way up to a production manager. After completing my Associates Degree at Wake Tech I transferred to NC State to complete my bachelors degree. During the summer between Wake Tech and NC State I worked for a consulting firm called BNK Engineering. I worked as a drafter and a surveyor as an intern working for new and existing construction.
